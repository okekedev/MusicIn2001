# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 2025**

## Overview

MyMusic - 2001 ("the App") is developed by Christian Okeke. This policy describes how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.** The App operates entirely on your device and your personal iCloud account.

### What the App Accesses

- **Your Music Files**: Stored locally on your Mac and synced via your personal iCloud account
- **iCloud Storage**: Used solely to sync your music library between your own devices
- **Device Battery Level**: Displayed in the iOS app interface (not transmitted anywhere)

### What We Do NOT Collect

- No analytics or tracking
- No personal information
- No usage data
- No advertising identifiers
- No third-party SDKs that collect data

## iCloud Sync

The App uses Apple's iCloud to sync your music library between your Mac and iOS devices. This data:
- Is stored in your personal iCloud account
- Is governed by Apple's privacy policy
- Is not accessible to us

## Third-Party Services

The Mac app can download audio from external URLs. These downloads are processed locally on your device. We do not operate any servers or collect any information about your downloads.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
