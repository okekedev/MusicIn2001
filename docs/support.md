# Support

## How to Use MyMusic - 2001

### Getting Started

1. **Download MyMusic on your Mac** from the Mac App Store
2. **Add songs** by dragging MP3 files or pasting a link
3. **Open MyMusic on your iPhone**
4. **Tap Sync** in the Settings menu - your library will appear

### Mac App

**Adding Music**
- Drag and drop MP3 files into the app
- Paste a link and click Download
- Files are automatically organized by Artist/Album

**Creating Playlists**
- Go to Playlists in the sidebar
- Click + to create a new playlist
- Drag songs into your playlist

**Using the Mixer**
- Switch to the Mix tab
- Load tracks onto each deck
- Use the crossfader to blend between tracks
- Adjust EQ and volume per deck

### iOS App

**Navigation**
- Rotate your finger on the click wheel to scroll
- Tap the center button to select
- Tap Menu (top) to go back
- Tap Play/Pause (bottom) to control playback
- Tap Previous/Next (left/right) to change tracks

**Syncing**
- Make sure you're signed into iCloud on both devices
- On iOS, go to Settings > Sync
- Wait for your library to download

### Troubleshooting

**Music not syncing?**
- Ensure both devices use the same iCloud account
- Check your iCloud storage isn't full
- Try tapping Sync again on iOS

**Background playback stopped?**
- Make sure the app wasn't force-closed
- Check that Low Power Mode isn't restricting background activity
